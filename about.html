Process of Paddy

Paddy is a raw form of rice. It has certain methods to process the paddy and to achieve perfect shape, size of rice. The milled rice is the primary process of removal of husk, bran and germs. This helps us to prevent the spoilage of rice and extend the texture, flavor, appearance and storage life of rice. It is further processed to reach the secondary and tertiary products.

The basic milled rice process consist of:

Pre-Cleaning
Paddy cleaner is a mandatory equipment in the rice mill. It segregates all the impurities such as clay, straw, dust, sand and heavy impurities from paddy. The paddy cleaner increases the life of rubber rollers and the percentage of oil in bran.

Parboiling
This helps in improving the nutritional quality by gelatinization of starch inside the rice grain. It improves the milling a recovery percent during deshelling and polishing/whitening operation.

De-Stoning
De-stoning is the process of removing the tiny stones from the paddy.

Hulling
Dehuller is used to dehusk the paddy and separate the kernel.

Husk Aspiration
It is a seperation of unhusked paddy from brown rice.

Whitening
Removal of germs from brown rice. the process of whitening is to convert from Brown rice to White rice. We use three whitener techniques by making less chemical and less polish to make healthier rice and thus the rice is whitened very gently.

Polishing
It improves the appearance of milled rice by polishing the milled kernels.

Length Grading
It is to separate the broken rice from the head rice.
